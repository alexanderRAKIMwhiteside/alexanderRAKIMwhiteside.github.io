“One Message” Infographic Poster

Project Details

Client: Course assignment

Medium: Digital Print

Tools: Adobe InDesign

Field: Graphic Design, Information Design, Typography

Objective: Create connection between Martin Luther’s goals of reformation and present day. Martin Luther wanted the Bible to be accessible to people in their commonly spoken language just as Wycliffe Bible Translators aim to translate the Bible for every language today.
