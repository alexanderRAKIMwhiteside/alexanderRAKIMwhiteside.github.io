Hospital of Hope; Mango, Togo: Visual Identity

Project Details

Client: Course Assignment, Hospital of Hope; Mango, Togo

Medium: Digital Print

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign

Field: Graphic Design, Print Design, Typography, Logo Design, Branding

Objective: Create a visual identity for Hospital of Hope in Mango, Togo in West Africa. Mark and accompanying sationery needed to communicate essence and uniqueness of organization in a way that Americans could relate to.
