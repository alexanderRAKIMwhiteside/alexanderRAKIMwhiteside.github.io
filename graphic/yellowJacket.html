“The Yellow Jacket” Self-Mailer

Project Details

Client: Course Assignment, Cedarville University Admission and Marketing Departments

Medium: Digital Print

Tools: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop

Field: Graphic Design, Print Design, Illustration, Editorial Design

Objective: Work with a small creative team (myself with three others) to create a machineable mailer to send to juniors or seniors in high school expressing initial interest in gaining more information from Cedarville.
